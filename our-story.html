My Journey

HOW IT ALL STARTED

I've always found skincare very fascinating since childhood. Watching Youtube videos and seeing the way girls' skin would glow from using face products tempted me to save every buck I had and walk to the nearest Target or Walmart to buy a face mask, face wash, or acne treatment. I would run home and quickly indulge my skin in the products. It was so exhilarating to have what I thought was a luxurious product on my skin.

The products gave a tingling sensation making me feel like my skin was undergoing a magical spell to reveal flawless skin that shimmers with dewy radiance like the girls I saw on Youtube.

Oh, was I wrong.

My skin became far from flawless. My cheeks were suffused with blemishes and painful, inflamed red-ringed pustules.

Personal Experience

MY SKINCARE JOURNEY

Growing up as a person of color with very dry skin and severe acne, there weren't many products on the shelves that could tackle my skin issues. The few products that were on the shelves or that dermatologists recommended to me had no beneficial impact on my face.

I lost all hope of improving my skin and my self confidence worsened. The humiliation and dirty looks from other people was unbearable. The fear that boys would not be interested in me until the Gusher-like bumps on my face popped pushed me to take initiative to intensively research.

I made products that helped me cure & restore my skin. Not only did it restore my skin, but it restored my happiness and gave me back my confidence!

Our Mission

WELCOME TO COLDGLOWCOSMETICS!

YOUR SKIN'S BEST FRIEND

I wanted to help others who were also going through skin problems and had hard times finding products that actually worked for them!

Each formula is the result of intensive research and personal experience, created to deliver real results without harmful ingredients or false promises.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do, from product development to customer service.

Natural Ingredients

We believe in harnessing the power of nature. Our products feature carefully selected natural ingredients that deliver real results without compromising your skin's health.

Scientific Approach

While we love natural ingredients, we also believe in science. Our formulations are backed by research and testing to ensure they're not only safe but truly effective.

Cruelty-Free

We're proudly cruelty-free. None of our products or ingredients are tested on animals, and we partner only with suppliers who share this commitment.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through sustainable sourcing, eco-friendly packaging, and responsible manufacturing practices.

Community Focus

We believe in building a community of empowered individuals who celebrate their unique beauty. We listen to our customers and grow with their feedback.

Transparency

We're open about our ingredients, processes, and practices. We believe you have the right to know exactly what you're putting on your skin.

Our Journey

Key Moments in Our Story

From kitchen experiments to award-winning skincare, here's how GLOW Beauty grew over the years.

2018

The Beginning

Emma Thompson started creating her first formulations in her kitchen, focused on solving her own skincare struggles with natural ingredients.

2019

First Product Launch

After months of testing and refinement, our first three products were launched to friends and family, receiving overwhelming positive feedback.

2020

Online Store Launch

GLOW Beauty officially launched online with a collection of five signature products, gaining attention on social media for our natural approach.

2021

Expanded Product Line

We expanded our collection to include targeted solutions for different skin concerns, introducing serums and specialized treatments.

2022

Beauty Award Winner

Our Golden Hour Body Scrub won "Best Natural Exfoliator" at the Annual Beauty Innovation Awards, putting us on the map nationally.

2023

Retail Partnerships

GLOW Beauty products became available in select boutique retailers across the country, expanding our reach to new customers.

2024

New Headquarters

We moved into our new eco-friendly headquarters with an expanded lab and testing facilities to continue innovating our product line.

2025

Today & Beyond

With a growing team of skincare experts and a devoted community, we continue to create innovative products while staying true to our founding principles.
