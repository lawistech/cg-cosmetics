Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products and services.

How long does shipping take?

Please allow 7-14 business days for us to process your order. After that, shipping time depends on the shipping method you selected at checkout. Business days do not include weekends and holidays. Please note that shipping may take longer than usual due to COVID-19.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally. Please note that international customers are responsible for any customs fees and import duties that may be charged upon delivery. These fees are not included in the purchase price.

What is your return policy?

Due to the nature of our products, all sales are final. We do not accept returns or exchanges. Prior to making your purchase, please review the product details carefully to ensure it's right for you.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all our products are cruelty-free. We do not test on animals, and we do not use ingredients that have been tested on animals. We are committed to ethical practices in all aspects of our business.

Do you offer sample sizes?

Currently, we do not offer sample sizes of our products. However, we do occasionally include free samples with orders over a certain amount. Sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about these promotions.
